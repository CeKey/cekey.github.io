3. Data Collection and Processing

3.1 Data Collected by the Developer

The developer does not collect any personal data.

  • No user accounts are created.
  • No precise location data (GPS) is accessed.
  • No camera or microphone access is required.
  • No data is stored on the developer’s servers.
  • Local Data: Settings such as the last selected color remain exclusively on your device.

3.2 Data Collected by Third Parties (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob (Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland) to display full-screen advertisements (Interstitials).

AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Identifiers: Advertising ID (GAID), Android ID
  • Device Info: Model, operating system version, screen size
  • Network Info: IP address, network type (Wi-Fi/Mobile)
  • Usage Behavior: App interactions, ad clicks, and view duration
  • Approximate Location: derived from the IP address (not precise)

Legal Basis: Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR (Consent) or Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR (Legitimate Interest).

4. Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) / Consent

The app utilizes the Google UMP SDK (v3.2.0) to obtain user consent in accordance with the GDPR (EEA/UK) and CCPA/CPRA (California).

Upon the first launch, a consent dialog provided by Google will appear. Google AdMob is only initialized after you have interacted with this dialog. You can choose to accept or decline personalized advertising. If declined, ads will still be shown, but they will be non-personalized.

5. App Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • INTERNET: To load and display advertisements
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check if a connection is available for ads
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: To provide network information to the AdMob SDK

6. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

This app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data via the Advertising ID, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Data collected by Google AdMob may be transferred to the United States. Google LLC is certified under the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, ensuring an adequate level of data protection.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & US Privacy)

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data.

Opt-Out: You can reset or disable your Advertising ID at any time via: Android Settings > Google > Ads > Reset/Delete Advertising ID.

Deletion: Since we do not store data on our own servers, you can exercise your “Right to be Forgotten” by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data in your system settings.

9. Important Safety Warning (Epilepsy)

WARNING: This app generates flashing strobe light effects. Persons with epilepsy or photosensitivity should not use this app. Usage is at your own risk.

3. App Permissions & Technical Data

To function correctly and display advertisements, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Advertising IDs & Attribution (AD_ID, ACCESS_ADSERVICES_...): These allow access to the Android Advertising ID and Google’s Privacy Sandbox. They are used to measure ad performance and, subject to consent, display personalized ads.
  • Network & Internet (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): Necessary to load ad content from Google servers and verify the app's license.
  • License Check (CHECK_LICENSE): Used to verify that the app was legitimately acquired via the Google Play Store.
  • Background Processing (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, WAKE_LOCK): These ensure that game progress (Idle mechanics) is processed even if the app briefly enters the background or the screen dims.
